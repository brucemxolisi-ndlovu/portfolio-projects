About Us

At iSBK, your safety comes first. We provide smart, reliable security solutions that give homeowners peace of mind and help businesses protect what matters most. Our approach is built on three pillars: Distinct in how we think, Resolute in our commitment, and Relevant in adapting to today's evolving risks.


From homes to businesses, we aim to be the frontline between you and risk—so you can focus on living and working with confidence. Inspired by our name Isibuko ("Mirror"), iSBK reflects the trust, strength, and protection our clients want to see in their security partner.

Distinct

The company was founded on an audacious way of thinking. We have since embraced boldness and are unafraid to be different in our approach to security solutions.

Resolute

We are firm in our commitment to providing resilient and competent security solutions with the aid of our 3 pillars by which our services are governed.

Relevant

We continuously adapt and develop our strategies to stay ahead of potential threats, allowing iSBK to provide competent security service.

Our Story

iSBK has been an idea in development and honing dating back to 2013. Initially conceptualized as Isibuko InServe, and later Isibuko Umbrella. After numerous changes and unsuccessful partnerships, with each challenge adding strength to our resolve, we proudly present iSBK – an entity honed to its most robust, ready to compete and serve the Republic of South Africa.
