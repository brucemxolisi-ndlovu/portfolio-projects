Understanding Our Community Safety Platform

Our Mission

Creating Safer Communities Through Technology

LiveGuard exists to create safer, more connected communities through technology and human collaboration. We believe that when neighbors look out for each other, communities thrive.

Emergency SOS

Instant alert system that notifies your trusted contacts and nearby community members. Includes location sharing and real-time updates.

Missing Persons Alerts

Quick distribution of critical information across the community network when time matters most. Includes photo sharing and last-known location mapping.

Community Crime Watch

Collaborative tool for neighborhood watch groups to coordinate efforts and share real-time updates about suspicious activities.

Local Business Directory

Curated list of verified local businesses and emergency services, ensuring you always have quick access to help when needed.

How It Works

  1. Join your local LiveGuard network
  2. Connect with verified neighbors
  3. Set up your emergency contacts
  4. Receive real-time community alerts
  5. Participate in making your community safer
