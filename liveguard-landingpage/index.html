About LiveGuard

LiveGuard's mission is to empower neighbourhoods with simple, reliable tools to share alerts, report incidents, and support one another in times of need. We focus on real-world community safety: fast alerts, clear communication, and local action.

  • Missing persons alerts — Share critical details widely and quickly.
  • Emergency SOS — One-touch alerts with location sharing.
  • Local business directory — Trusted local services and partners.
  • Quick report — Report incidents to your community and get help fast.
