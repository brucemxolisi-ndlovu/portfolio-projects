About Zambezi Capital

Disciplined Investing.
Exceptional Results.

Zambezi Capital Partners is a boutique investment firm specializing in high-conviction, research-driven strategies across public and private markets. We partner with sophisticated investors to deliver superior risk-adjusted returns through rigorous analysis, disciplined execution, and long-term alignment.

Research-Driven

Proprietary analysis and deep fundamental research power every decision.

Learn More

Our investment process is grounded in exhaustive primary research, forensic accounting, and proprietary modeling. We seek asymmetric opportunities where market mispricing creates durable alpha, with a relentless focus on downside protection.

Risk First

Capital preservation is non-negotiable. Returns follow discipline.

Learn More

We believe consistent outperformance begins with avoiding permanent loss. Every position undergoes stress testing across multiple scenarios. Risk management is embedded in our DNA — not an afterthought.

Aligned Partnership
